'Design that works' is QubStudio tag line. The digital design company based in Lviv, Ukraine, currently employs 25 people, and its work is mostly on UX/UI design. QubStudio is the brainchild of Lyubomyr Koval, a web developer, and Victor Fedyuk, a designer and artist. With a mutual passion for contemporary web design and a shared business sense, the friends decided to get together and fund the company over seven years ago. First there were two; now they are 25 and only last year they moved into a bigger office to allow for more growth and creativity.

he hard working Ukrainian team knows that great things don't 'just happen.' On its website, it lists a code of rules, which it uses to achieve what its customers want: creative and effective user interfaces. The page opens with a quote from contemporary Artist Andrew Hendrixson: 'Anyone who has ever made anything of importance was disciplined.'
